Welcome to Pets-R-Us. We've been rescuing dogs and helping them find forever homes for what seems like forever. Since we opened our doors we've been one of the largest pet stores to our local community. Because of this and the support of everyone, we've expanded our outreach and have opened another store. We've also recently expanded our operation to include new services like training, grooming, and boarding. This would not have been made possible without the love and support of everyone who visits us, the communities we serve, and our love and passion for our four-legged friends.

Dog of the Week

Meet Dixie. Dixie is a fun-loving puppy of 5 months old. She earned a spot this week on our list for graduating top in her class in the beginners category of our training classes. Dixie's favorite thing to do is chew on her toys. Her momma learned very fast that Dixie is a big time chewer. Now Dixie has more toys than there are furniture in the house. Not only that, but Dixie learned all the beginner commands and actions that we teach in our puppy beginner training classes and she did it with almost no fuss. We look forward to seeing Dixie grow and hope to see her in the upcoming puppy intermediate classes.
