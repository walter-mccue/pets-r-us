Bath Time

Washing a dog can be tough sometimes. No matter the reason, that’s one more reason we are here. As long as you can provide vaccination records, we’ll take care of your fur baby. Just be sure to let us know of any allergies. All dog baths consist of a shampoo tailored to their fur needs. Short haired breeds get an oatmeal-based shampoo that assists in shedding the undercoat. Long haired dogs get a plant-based shampoo/conditioner combination to assist with the silky-smooth coat. Dogs with skin-conditions and allergies get a medical grade shampoo to assist with healing and moisturization. All baths end with a gentle blow dry and brush. We don't up-charge for breeds or large dogs.

Service Price
Bath $20


Haircuts

Just like washing a dog, giving them a haircut can be tough. But we’ve got that covered too. Whether it’s a simple trim, shave, or a custom look you are going for, we have specialists who can make it happen. Pricing on haircuts is a bit different than pricing on baths but we still don't charge based on size or breed.
